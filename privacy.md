# Privacy Policy for Mitady

**Effective date:** April 18, 2026
**Last updated:** April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mitady ("we," "our," "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our bill-splitting mobile application.

Mitady is developed by Antonio Mass, based in Mexico. If you have any questions or concerns about this policy or your data, contact us at **antoniomassencinas@gmail.com**.

---

## 1. Information we collect

When you use Mitady, we collect the following information:

### 1.1 Account information
When you sign in with Apple, Google, or email, we receive and store:
- Your name (as provided by the sign-in provider)
- Your email address
- A profile photo, if your provider supplies one
- A unique user identifier assigned by the sign-in provider

We do not store your password. Authentication is handled entirely by Apple, Google, or our authentication provider (Firebase Authentication).

### 1.2 Content you create
To provide Mitady's core bill-splitting functionality, we store:
- Groups you create or join (group name, currency, emoji)
- Member names you add to groups
- Expenses you record (description, amount, payer, participants, date)
- Settlement records between group members
- Optional receipt photos you attach to expenses

### 1.3 Technical information
We collect standard technical data required to operate the app reliably:
- App version
- Device operating system (iOS version)
- Crash reports and error logs (via Firebase Crashlytics, if enabled)
- Anonymous usage metrics (via Firebase Analytics, if enabled)

We do not collect your precise location, contact list, camera roll (beyond photos you explicitly attach), microphone audio, or health data.

---

## 2. How we use your information

We use the information above solely to:
- Provide the bill-splitting features you request
- Synchronize your data across your devices
- Enable group members you invite to see shared group content
- Diagnose crashes and improve app stability
- Communicate with you about your account when necessary

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not use it to train AI models.

---

## 3. Who can see your information

- **You** can see all information associated with your account.
- **Members of a group you create or join** can see the group name, member names you added to that group, and expenses within that group.
- **Our service providers** (Google Firebase, Apple) process your data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
- **Legal authorities** only when required by valid legal process.

Mitady has no employees other than the developer. No employee, contractor, or third party accesses your content except as necessary to operate the service.

---

## 4. How we store and protect your data

Your data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure in servers located in the United States and/or European Union. Firebase encrypts data in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256).

We do not have access to your sign-in credentials. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device by the operating system.

---

## 5. Your rights

Regardless of where you live, you have the right to:

- **Access** a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correct** any inaccurate personal data.
- **Delete** your account and all associated data. You can do this directly from the app's Settings screen. Deletion is permanent and takes effect within 30 days.
- **Export** your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Withdraw consent** at any time by deleting your account.

### For users in the European Union (GDPR)
Our legal basis for processing your data is **contract performance** (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR) — we process your data to provide the service you requested. You have additional rights under GDPR including lodging a complaint with your local data protection authority.

### For users in California (CCPA/CPRA)
California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. We do not "sell" personal information as defined by the CCPA.

### For users in Mexico (LFPDPPP)
You have ARCO rights (Acceso, Rectificación, Cancelación, Oposición) under the Ley Federal de Protección de Datos Personales en Posesión de los Particulares. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

To exercise any of these rights, email **antoniomassencinas@gmail.com**. We respond within 30 days.

## 6. Children

Mitady is not directed at children under 13 years of age (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction — 16 in parts of the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it.

## 7. Data retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal information and content you created is permanently deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (for example, financial or tax records).

Anonymized technical logs may be retained longer for security and abuse detection purposes.
